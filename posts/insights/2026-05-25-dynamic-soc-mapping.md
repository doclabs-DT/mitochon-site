### The Science: Two Structural Shifts Reshaping the Field

Right now, the core treatment algorithms for TNBC are restructuring in real-time. If you look closely at the charts, you can see exactly where the frontline metastatic and early-stage strategies are being redrawn.

### 1. The Frontline Metastatic Reset: Enter Dato-DXd

For years, roughly 70% of patients presenting with frontline metastatic TNBC were locked out of standard immunotherapy because of low PD-L1 expression (CPS < 10) or prior exposure during their early-stage treatment. For these patients, standard cytotoxic chemotherapy was the default, uninspiring backbone.

* **The Friday Surprise:** On **May 22, 2026**, the FDA disrupted that paradigm by approving **Datopotamab deruxtecan (Dato-DXd)** for adults with unresectable or metastatic TNBC who aren't candidates for PD-1/PD-L1 inhibitors.
* **The Clinical Catalyst:** This decision was catalyzed by the Phase III **TROPION-Breast02** trial, where Dato-DXd posted a median Progression-Free Survival (PFS) of 10.8 months against chemotherapy’s 5.6 months, pushing Overall Survival (OS) out to 23.7 months.
* **The Domino Effect:** In **Diagram 1**, this forces an immediate rewrite of the **"TL Metastatic PD-L1 neg/ineligible"** pathway. It abruptly yanks the baseline away from traditional single-agent chemos and establishes preferred antibody-drug conjugate (ADC) sequencing right at the starting line.

### 2. Early-Stage Durability: Raising the Bar

Meanwhile, the curative setting is cementing benchmarks that alter the downstream volume of recurrent disease.

* **The High-Water Mark:** Freshly updated 7-year data from the landmark **KEYNOTE-522** trial has locked in a **7-year Event-Free Survival (EFS) rate of 78.3%** for perioperative neoadjuvant pembrolizumab + chemo.

**The Downstream Impact:** Because more patients are achieving a Pathological Complete Response (pCR), the pool of patients moving into the adjuvant phase with residual disease (the non-pCR track in the upper right quadrant of the maps) is shrinking but becoming intensely complex. This directly alters how teams calculate downstream market sizing for metastatic assets.
