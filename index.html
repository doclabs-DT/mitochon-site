The Mito Advantage

Why generic AI cannot solve this

Four structural problems in oncology intelligence that general-purpose LLMs cannot address — and how Mito AI solves each one.

01
The Lexicon Challenge
Mito Answer
Different names, acronyms and contexts for the same clinical concept. Mito AI resolves this through entity normalisation and disambiguation across the full oncology evidence base — unified in our knowledge mesh.
02
The Relevance Problem
Mito Answer
Evidence only matters in a specific setting, line, population and biomarker context. We decompose science into atomic units and layer proprietary data constructs that encode relevance into every answer.
03
The Hierarchy Problem
Mito Answer
A pivotal Phase 3 trial and a congress poster should not carry equal weight. Mito AI grounds synthesis in ranked evidence, source audit and expert review — so outputs reflect the actual evidence hierarchy.
04
The Governance Problem
Mito Answer
High-stakes outputs need transparency, control, versioning and reusability. Verisight delivers editable, auditable, shareable workflows that compound institutional knowledge across every function.
