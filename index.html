Our Philosophy

AI that augments expertise.
Not replaces it.

We build at the intersection of generative and agentic AI — and we are deliberate about where each belongs. Generative AI accelerates synthesis. Agentic AI handles orchestration. Human expertise — the oncologist, the MSL, the strategist — remains in charge of what matters: judgment, context and the decision itself.

We don't believe AI should replace scientific expertise in pharma R&D. The complexity of oncology, the stakes of the decisions, and the regulatory environment all demand a human who understands the science and owns the process as much as the outcome. Our role is to make that human faster, better-informed and more defensible — not to remove them from the loop. With Verisight, the user is in control throughout.

That means striking the right balance between control and delegation at every layer of the platform. We'd love to share how we think about that.
